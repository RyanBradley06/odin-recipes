Home Made Pizza

Ingredients:

For the Dough:

  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast (1 packet)
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water (about 110째F or 45째C)
  • 3 1/2 to 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt

For the toppings:

  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce or pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your choice of toppings (e.g., pepperoni, sliced bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, etc.)
  • Italian seasoning (optional)
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the dough:

    • In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, instant yeast, sugar, and salt.

    • Make a well in the center and pour in lukewarm water and olive oil.

    • Stir until a dough forms.

    • Transfer the dough onto a floured surface and knead for about 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.

    • Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a kitchen towel or plastic wrap
      and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-2 hours or until doubled in size.
